Thank you for downloading this sample pack, I hope you enjoy it, and that it may be of some use to you. If so, please consider
tipping me if you can, I'd really appreciate it.

The range of samples is G2 to C5, although I've programmed Decent Sampler to artificially extend the top and bottom notes to fill
the entire keyboard because, well, why not? The default settings are good for dramatic volume swells and the like, although you could
also try reducing the reverb level and upping the release time for a more intimate sound should you so desire.
